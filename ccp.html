Towards Precision Medicine into Colorectal Cancer

Python/Machine Learning


Description:
Precision medicine is the future! It involves using genetic information and physiological conditions to tailor diagnosis, treatment plans, and prevention strategies to individual patients.


Question:
Given the labor-intensive and variable nature of annotating microscopic images, how can we improve the accuracy and consistency of histological analysis to better treat colorectal cancer?


Solution:
By automating the classification of colorectal tissue types, we can streamline the histopathological analysis process, reduce diagnostic variability, and improve the reproducibility of results.


Utilization:

  • CNNs (Convolutional Neural Networks)
  • Neural Network Training
  • Train-Test Split
  • Evaluation Metrics
