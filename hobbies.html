My Hobbies & Interests

1. Coding and Game Development

One of my biggest passions is coding, especially when it comes to creating interactive games. I love to dive deep into Python and other languages, crafting fun experiences like the **Runner-Snail-Jump** game. Building games has helped me hone my problem-solving skills and creativity, allowing me to bring my ideas to life through technology.


2. Reading

I'm an avid reader, especially of realisitic fiction and speculative fiction. I enjoy exploring futuristic worlds, imagining what technology and society might look like in the years to come. This genre has always been a source of inspiration for my personal projects and keeps my curiosity alive.


3. Playing and Learning New Musical Instruments

Music has always been a form of relaxation for me. I enjoy playing the piano and the guitar in my spare time, experimenting with different styles and melodies. Recently, I’ve been learning new instruments, which gives me a refreshing break from coding while fueling my creativity in other areas. I love playing music, listening to music, and song writing. I play the saxophone, bass guitar, keyboard, viola, acoustic guitar, and I used to play the clarinet. Music has always been an integral part of my life and will remain one.


4. Skateboarding

Skateboarding is one of my favorite ways to stay active and have fun. I enjoy practicing new tricks and cruising around town, feeling the rush of excitement when I land something challenging. Skateboarding helps me stay physically fit while offering a break from my daily routine, giving me a perfect balance between work and play.


5. Cooking

I love spending time in the kitchen, experimenting with new recipes and cooking techniques. Cooking allows me to combine creativity with practicality, and it’s always satisfying to share a good meal with friends or family. Whether I'm baking pastries or trying out international dishes, the kitchen is a place where I can unwind and express myself.
